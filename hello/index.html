About Me

I’ve spent a lot of time trying to be a better teammate — that means getting to know my strengths (and weaknesses, too). I’m a INTJ, an Enneagram 8, and my StrengthsFinder top five are Input, Command, Futuristic, Intellection, and Communication.

INTJs are naturally strategic thinkers — imaginative, decisive, and infinitely curious. We like to master areas of study, craft contingency plans, and constantly assess new tactics and strategies. You know, real Batman in his secret Batcave stuff.

Enneagram 8s are challengers — straight-talking, resourceful, and assertive. We have a passionate inner drive, a desire to champion the people around us, and an enterprising, pragmatic work ethic. Hemingway, Picasso, Queen Latifah, and the Godfather of Soul himself, Mr. James Brown.

My strengths are focused on building the future — collecting and connecting the dots, casting compelling vision, making strategic decisions, and communicating a path forward. Being a part of a team with diverse opinions is key to making sure that future is well-informed, empathetic, and human-centered.
